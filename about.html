About the project

As associated with the name of the course, this project is meant to give us an opportunity to learns the ins and outs of web development. This is done by creating a research website based off a
research topic of choice. The topic I chose to research was the history and usage of the Uniform Resource Locator (URL). From the tabs above, they're dedicated pages around the topic, key concepts and
references that allow users to gain an understanding of the topic. The website also serves as a milestone for our learning as the website evolves overtime as we incorporate new concepts and criteria to the website.
